## Key Features

### Portfolio Management
- Max 5 simultaneous positions
- 8 sectors: BTC, ETH, L1, L2, DEFI, OLD, MEME, CEX
- Correlation filter (threshold 0.7)
- Portfolio heat tracking (max 8% total risk)
- Position sizing with volatility adjustment

### Live Engine
- WebSocket integration for real-time data
- Signal generation on closed candles
- Automatic TP/SL tracking with cascade stops
- State persistence in SQLite database
- Crash recovery with position restoration

### Risk Management
- Fixed percent risk per trade (default 2%)
- Kelly criterion support
- Volatility-adjusted position sizing
- Sector diversification limits
